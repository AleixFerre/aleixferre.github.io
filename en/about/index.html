Who am I?

I'm a Game Design and Development studient in University of Girona. I have facility with video game programming and design.

GitHub itch.io

I'm studying a Bachelor's degree in Design and Development of Video Games in University of Girona. Besides that, you can see all the applications I made on my own in my GitHub account. My main hobby is the game development and I have collaborated on several projects with Unity and Unreal Engine (although I prefer Unity).

My favourite part of the creation of a video game is the programming and the design of mechanics and levels. Whenever I have a mechanic in mind, I want to open Unity ASAP and start testing if it works.

I have participated in several GameJams and they were very entertaining and fun experiences doing simple but effective video games. The games that I have made in GameJams are (ordered the most new first): Drag them out into space!, SmokeFighter and Throw 88
